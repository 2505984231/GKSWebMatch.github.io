Detailed introduction

The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge is located between the Snake Mountain in Wuchang District of Wuhan City and the Guishan Mountain in Hanyang, Hubei Province. It is the first bridge on the Yangtze River and the first public railway bridge built on the Yangtze River after the founding of New China. "The first bridge of the Yangtze River." When the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge was completed, it became the landmark building of Wuhan.


The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge was one of the 156 projects aided by the Soviet Union. It was started in September 1955 and officially opened to traffic on October 15, 1957. The total length is about 1670 meters. The upper level is the highway bridge (107 national highway), and the lower level is the double-track railway bridge (Beijing-Guangzhou Railway). The bridge has a total of 8 piers and 9 holes. The span of each hole is 128 meters. In addition to the 7th pier, the 8 piers use the “large pipe string drilling method”, which is a new construction method pioneered by China.


The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge connects the three towns of Wuhan into one, which greatly promotes the economic and social development of Wuhan. At the same time, the bridge will be connected by the Jinghan Railway separated by the Yangtze River and the Yuehan Railway, thus forming a complete Beijing-Guangzhou railway, which plays an important role in promoting the economic development of China's north and south.


In June 1956, Mao Zedong wrote in the "Water-sounding Head and Swimming", "One Bridge Fly North and South, Tianzhu Change Path" is a true portrayal of the important role of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge in communicating China's north-south traffic. As the main achievement of China's first five-year plan, the bridge pattern was selected into the third set of RMB issued in April 1962, which became an important symbol of the construction of the new China.


Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge is one of the famous tourist attractions in China. On May 3, 2013, Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge became the seventh batch of national key cultural relics protection units. In September 2016, it was selected as the “First China 20th Century Architectural Heritage” list.


architectural design

The bridge is a highway and railway dual-purpose bridge, the upper level is a highway, two-way four-lane, sidewalks on both sides; the lower level is a double-track railway. The total length of the whole bridge is 1670 meters, including 1156 meters for the main bridge, 303 meters for the northwest shore approach bridge and 211 meters for the southeast shore approach bridge. It is 80 meters high from the base to the bridge deck, and the lower level is a two-lane railway bridge with a width of 14.5 meters. The two trains can be opened at the same time. The upper level is a highway bridge, 22.5 meters wide, of which: the roadway is 18 meters, with 4 lanes; the sidewalks on both sides of the roadway are 2.25 meters. The bridge is a triple continuous bridge with 3 holes each, a total of 8 piers and 9 holes. The span of each hole is 128 meters, which plays a big role in the unimpeded sailing of the whole year.


Technological innovation

The former Soviet Union expert Xilin proposed the idea of drilling the foundation of the pipe string, which is to drive the hollow pipe string into the rock face of the riverbed and drill holes in the rock face, and inject concrete into the hole to firmly insert it into the rock. Then build the cap and pier on it. This is a completely innovative technology. The technical staff of the two countries worked closely together and after more than a year of geological surveys and painstaking experimental research, they finally decided to use this technology.


Because of the use of the world's most advanced construction method at the time, the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge was originally planned to be completed in 4 years and 1 month, and it only took 2 years and 1 month.


Construction significance

Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge is the first bridge built on the Yangtze River after the founding of New China. It is also the first bridge on the Yangtze River from ancient times to the present. It is the first double-track railway and highway bridge in China. After the completion of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge It has become an important artery connecting the north and south of China and has played an important role in promoting the economic development of the North and the South. The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge embodies the ingenuity of the designers and the craftsmanship of the builders. It is not only a beautiful scenery on the Yangtze River, but also a historical monument, leaving an indelible impression on the lives of people in Jiangcheng.
