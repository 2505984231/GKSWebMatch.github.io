Yingkou Port

Introduction

The geographic coordinates of Yingkou Port are 40°17′42′′ north latitude and 122°06′00′′ east longitude. It is an important comprehensive hub port in the country. It is the nearest port in the northeast and eastern Inner Mongolia, and the largest cargo port in the northeast. , the core port of the Liaodong Bay Economic Zone.

Yingkou Port is operated by Yingkou Port Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as the company). It has jurisdiction over Yingkou Port Area, Bayuquan Port Area, Xianren Island Port Area, Panjin Port Area, Ocean Honggang District, Yuzhong Shihe Port Area and Huludao Witch Valley Port Area. . In 2012, the throughput was 30.07 million tons, and the throughput ranked 9th in the national coastal area.

Yingkou Port

history
Yingkou Port Co., Ltd. was registered and established on March 22, 2000 in Liaoning Provincial Administration for Industry and Commerce with a registered capital of RMB 150 million. At the end of March 2001, the company successfully passed the acceptance of the Shenyang Securities and Exchange Commission, and at the same time obtained the Liaoning Provincial Government listing recommendation letter, and passed the audit of the provincial government financial office. On October 19, 2001, the company's first application for stock is approved by the China Securities Regulatory Commission.
business
Yingkou Port loading, unloading, storage and transportation services. Run concurrently: steel structure engineering; machine parts processing and sales; port machinery, auto parts, steel, building materials, rubber products sales; production and sales of mattresses and labor insurance supplies; nylon rope production and sales; automobile repair, roller production and sales; Port crane transportation machinery equipment manufacturing installation and sales, belt conveyor, bucket turbine, trailer equipment installation and sales; lifting equipment maintenance, maintenance services; heating, property management.
Geographical advantage
With the advantage of natural conditions, the Bayuquan port area where the water is deep and small, does not freeze or freeze, and is open to the northeast of China. It has a geographical advantage. The Bayuquan Port Area is connected to the Shenyang-Dalian Expressway, the Harbin-Dalian Highway and the Changda Railway. The transportation is very convenient, and it has a strong ability to collect cargo. The port has a vast hinterland, the industrial and agricultural bases in the surrounding areas are relatively strong, and the business activities are frequent. It has a considerable scale of operation and has a high-quality talent team with a high level. The business capability has certain competitive advantages in terms of price, service quality, loading and unloading efficiency, and safety.
