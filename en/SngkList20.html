Nantong Tongzhou Bay Construction Jiangsu New Seaport Opening Hong Kong in 2022

Nantong Tongzhou Bay Construction Effect Map


People's Daily Online Nantong, July 9 (Reporter Wang Jiliang) Recently, the Jiangsu Provincial Party Committee and the Provincial Government issued the "Opinions on the Construction of the "One Belt, One Road" Meeting Point for High Quality Promotion", which put forward the clear requirements for "Building an Important Portal to the Sea" . On the 9th, Nantong held a press conference to announce the latest progress in the construction of the new seaport in Tongzhou Bay. On the basis of the approval of the reclamation, the start-up project of the Tongzhou Bay Port Area will be basically completed in 2022 to realize the port operation.

In addition, by 2025, the Tongzhou Bay Port Area will basically form a port infrastructure and a collection and distribution system with the functions of a container transportation hub. The shipping factor agglomeration and resource allocation capacity will be enhanced, and an important shipping hub in the lower reaches of the Yangtze River will be built, which will become a new container transportation in the Yangtze River. At the mouth of the sea; by 2035, it will become a demonstration zone for the integrated development of the province's ports, the core port of the North Wing hub of the Shanghai International Shipping Center, an important engine for the integrated development of the Yangtze River Delta, and an important support for the high-quality development of the Yangtze River Economic Belt. The container throughput capacity exceeds 15 million TEUs.

In order to make the orchard port of this group turn faster and more powerful, Chongqing Port Logistics Group Co., Ltd. has strategically integrated its Chongqing Orchard Port Container Terminal Co., Ltd. and Chongqing International Container Terminal Co., Ltd.

“At present, the Nantong Municipal Party Committee and the Municipal Government have set up the Tongzhou Bay Development and Construction Headquarters. The Jiangsu provincial level is also studying the institutional mechanism for promoting the construction of the new estuary of Tongzhou Bay, which will be established in accordance with the idea of “provincial leading and municipal entities”. At the provincial level, the leadership coordination agency.” Zhao Yufeng, deputy secretary general of Nantong Municipal Government, said that at present, the Tongzhou Bay City Master Plan (2018-2035) has been formed, the urban start-up area is basically delineated, and the urban construction three-year action plan, urban design and control The detailed regulations are being accelerated, and 44 urban construction projects implemented during the year have been fully launched.

It is understood that the construction of the new seaport mainly includes port engineering, collection and distribution engineering and shipping service construction. It will focus on the construction of three deep water channels, three river and sea transportation channels, three highway channels and two railway freight channels. Among them, the total investment of the first phase of the terminal project is about 8.8 billion yuan, and 10 50,000-ton berths in the port, four 100,000-ton in the second port, and two 200,000-ton berths in the three-port pool. The 50,000-ton terminal is expected to start in 2020 and will be put into operation in 2022. The total investment in the waterway project is about 15 billion yuan. The network of 100,000-ton waterway and breakwater of the net warehouse is expected to start construction in 2020, and will be completed before the end of 2022; the project of the 200,000-ton waterway upgrade of the net warehouse will start construction in 2022 and be completed before the end of 2024. The 300,000-tonne inbound waterway of Lengjiasha is being actively planned.

In June this year, the "Outline of the Yangtze River Delta Regional Integration Development Plan" issued by the State Council clearly stated that it plans to build a new seaport for the Yangtze River container transport in Tongzhou Bay, Nantong. The Tongshui Port Channel, Railway, Net Warehouse Hongway and Tongzhou Bay Expressway in Tongzhou Bay Port Area are included in the “Jiangsu Province Yangtze River Economic Belt Comprehensive Three-Dimensional Transportation Corridor Plan”, and the construction of the Tongzhou Bay Port Channel Terminal is included in the Ministry of Transport and the Yangtze River Delta. The six major action plans for the coordinated development of the integration of port and harbor in the Yangtze River Delta jointly issued by the three provinces and one city, the major projects of the distribution system, the Yanglu Railway and the Tongzhou Bay Shugang Railway were included in the provincial “13th Five-Year” railway plan.
