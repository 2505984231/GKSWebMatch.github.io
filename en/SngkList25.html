The cargo throughput of Zhenjiang Port has maintained growth for three consecutive years

In the first half of 2019, the number of international navigation vessels and the volume of foreign trade cargo in Zhenjiang Port reached a record high. On the 5th, the reporter learned from the Zhenjiang Maritime Safety Administration that as of June 30, the bureau handled 1,743 ships for import and export of international navigation ships, transporting goods of 15.52 million tons, and foreign trade cargo throughput increased by 10.5%. So far, the foreign trade cargo throughput in the first half of the year has maintained double-digit growth for three consecutive years.


The reporter learned that although the international situation has brought tremendous pressure on the development of foreign trade, the development of foreign trade in Zhenjiang Port has maintained a good momentum of development. Traditionally imported bulk goods still maintain steady growth with iron ore, coal and wood.


In order to support and promote local economic development, Zhenjiang Maritime Safety Bureau has continuously simplified the administrative examination and approval procedures and shortened the examination and approval time by actively implementing the “distribution service” measures. In particular, by accelerating the construction of brands such as “Beautiful Government Affairs Window” and “Jingjiang E Station Communication”, the procedures for the examination and approval of import and export shores are handled efficiently for international navigation vessels.


The relevant person in charge of Zhenjiang Maritime Safety Bureau told the reporter that the improvement of service efficiency and the optimization of service measures ensured the unimpeded access of the ship's import and export inspection “green channel” and promoted the rapid development of the export-oriented economy of Zhenjiang Port.
