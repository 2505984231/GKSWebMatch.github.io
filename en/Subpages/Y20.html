Technical characteristics

Pneumatic layout     The Yun-20 adopts the conventional layout. The wing is a cantilevered upper wing. The main wing has a large aspect ratio and a medium swept wing. The leading edge sweep angle of the wing is constant (the 1/4 string sweep angle is about 24~). Around 26 degrees), the trailing edge of the wing adopts two types of swept-back mode - the trailing edge sweep angle of the middle and outer wing sections is larger, and the sweep angle of the inner wing section of the wing is significantly reduced, and there is no winglet winglet. Cantilevered T-tail. There is a small dorsal fin forward at the joint between the vertical stabilizer and the fuselage.

The embedded rudder is divided into upper and lower sections, and the elevator is divided into two sections. The hydraulic retractable front three-point landing gear can be freely lowered by reliable gravity emergency.

Body structure     The Yun-20 uses supercritical wings and increases the proportion of composite materials used, which can reduce the structural weight of the aircraft. As the lift of the aircraft increases, the lift-to-drag ratio increases, so even if the engine thrust and the maximum take-off weight remain the same, the load will increase. The Il-76 uses a critical wing with a low carry/takeoff weight ratio.

Integrated avionics     Yun-20 adopts domestic integrated avionics system, with ARINC429 bus as the backbone, connects electronic flight instruments, flight control, navigation, communication, meteorological and engine data and instructions to achieve comprehensive data processing and color display in the cockpit. The screen is displayed. In addition, self-defense interference systems may be added, including missile approach warning systems, interference transmitters, and racks.
