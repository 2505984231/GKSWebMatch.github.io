Technical characteristics

"AG-600" is a comprehensive rescue aircraft developed and improved on the basis of the water bomber-5. It is about the same size as the A320. The maximum takeoff weight is 53.5 tons and the maximum range is 4500 kilometers. It is the world's largest amphibious aircraft that can take off and land on land and water, using three-person crews, two-person driving, and a mechanic.

The AG-600 aircraft adopts a large aspect ratio hull type, cantilevered trapezoidal upper wing; a "T" type rear wing, a front three-point retractable landing gear layout, an integrated avionics system, equipped with infrared detection and optical photography, etc. Search and detection equipment; emergency ambulance facilities equipped with water supply and water rescue, mainly including critically ill patients, ambulances, ambulances, stretchers, simple emergency surgery facilities and medicines.
