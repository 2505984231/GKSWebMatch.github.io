Qingdao Port

Qingdao Port is the port of Qingdao, Shandong Province, China. It is located on the bank of Jiaozhou Bay in Shandong Peninsula. It is across the sea from Japan and the Korean Peninsula. It is an international trade port and transit hub for China along the Yellow River Basin and the western coast of the Pacific Rim.

Shanghai Port

Shanghai Port is a port in Shanghai, China. It is located in the middle of the Chinese mainland coastline and at the mouth of the Yangtze River. It is connected to the north and south coasts of China and the world's oceans. It runs through the Yangtze River basin and the rivers and rivers of Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Taihu.

Yingkou Port

Yingkou Port is an important comprehensive main hub port in the country. It is the closest port to the Northeast region and the eastern part of Inner Mongolia, the largest cargo transportation port in the Northeast region, and the core port of the Liaodong Bay Economic Zone. The throughput is the 9th in the country.

Guangzhou Port

Guangzhou Port is the port of Guangzhou, Guangdong Province, China. It is located in the center of the Pearl River estuary and the Pearl River Delta. It is close to the South China Sea, adjacent to Hong Kong and Macao, and Dongjiang, Xijiang and Beijiang Rivers flow into the sea.

Tianjin Port

Tianjin Port, also known as Tianjin Xingang, is located at the mouth of the Haihe River in Tianjin, the People's Republic of China. It is at the intersection of the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei urban agglomeration and the Bohai Economic Circle. It is an important comprehensive port in northern China.

Dalian Port

Dalian Port, located in the Dalian Bay at the southern end of the Liaodong Peninsula, is the most convenient port for transshipment of Far East, South Asia, North America and Europe. It is one of the most important comprehensive foreign trade ports in the Northeast. It is a sea portal facing the world.
