Centennial airplane dream

Chinese aviation history

On September 21, 1909, China's first aircraft was successfully tested on the outskirts of Auckland, USA.

In August 1910, the Qing government allocated funds to build a factory shed in Beijing Nanyuan to build aircraft. And built the first airport in China.

September 1913 China's first aviation school, Beijing Nanyuan Aviation School, was established.

On April 24, 1920, China's first civil flight test flight was successful.

May 1, 1920 China's earliest civil aviation group was established in Beijing.
