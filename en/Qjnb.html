Well-known bridge selection

HK-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge

The Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge is a bridge and tunnel project connecting Hong Kong, Zhuhai and Macao in China. It is located in the Boyang area of Guangdong Province, China, and is the southern section of the expressway.

Detailed introduction

Hang-zhou Bay Bridge

The Hangzhou Bay Bridge is a cross-sea bridge connecting Jiaxing City and Ningbo City in Zhejiang Province, China. It is located on the sea area of Hangzhou Bay and is one of the components of Shenhai Expressway.

Detailed introduction

Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge

Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge is located in Wuhan City, Hubei Province. It is the first bridge on the Yangtze River and the first public railway bridge built on the Yangtze River after the founding of New China.

Detailed introduction

Nanjing Yangtze River Bridge

The Nanjing Yangtze River Bridge is the first double-deck railway and highway bridge designed and built by China on the Yangtze River. It is of great significance in the history of Chinese bridges.
