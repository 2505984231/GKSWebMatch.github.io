China Shanma Luzhou Port Station Xiamen-made unmanned vehicle leader

"Apolon" participated in the lead.

Xiamen Netcom (Xiamen Evening News correspondent Zhuang Xiaoling reporter Zhu Yasheng) On the 11th, 2018 China Mountain Marathon Series "Pisces Island Cup" Zhangzhou Port started in Zhangzhou Development Zone, attracting more than 4,000 athletes and mountain horse lovers from all over the country. participate.s

Together with Olympic champion Wang Junxia, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Mountaineering Association Wang Yongfeng, and Athletics World Championship champion Zhang Linli, there are also three globally-launched L4-class autonomous buses "Apollon". "Apolon" is manufactured by Xiamen Jinlong Automobile. Wang Yongfeng, vice chairman of the China Mountaineering Association, said that the unmanned car leading marathon has no precedent in the country.

The China Mountain Marathon Series has 10 stations this year. The Zhangzhou Port Station is the only station in Fujian and the only site with a beach track in China. It is known as the “Island Mountain Horse”. This track is a rare line of mountains, seas, lakes, islands, harbors, tunnels, reservoirs and customs towns. It is beautifully landscaped and is very popular among mountain horse athletes.s

After fierce competition, Yunnan’s selection of Dongjie won the first place in the men’s 42km individual, with a score of 3 hours, 04 minutes and 10 seconds. The first place in the women's team was won by Lu Yangchun, also from Yunnan, with a score of 3 hours, 37 minutes and 05 seconds.
