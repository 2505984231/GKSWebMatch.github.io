238 routes in Shenzhen Port reach the world, over 90% ULCS berthing

Established a friendly port with 17 internationally renowned ports

Shenzhen Port has 17 friendly ports and has carried out a series of cooperation and interaction. At the "21st Century Maritime Silk Road International Cooperation Port Forum and the 4th Shenzhen International Port Chain Strategy Forum" held in Shenzhen last year, many people expressed their concern about Shenzhen's construction of an international cooperation port. While consolidating the existing cooperation in the existing ports, Shenzhen Port has strengthened its port links with emerging markets such as Southeast Asia, the Middle East and West Africa.

　The largest cruise home port in South China

　According to the "Shenzhen Port Layout Plan" and "Shenzhen Port Shekou Passenger Port Area Plan", the Taiziwan area of Shekou Port Area of Shenzhen will be planned to be a sea-land-vacuum railway transshipment hub, an international cruise home port and a Hong Kong-Macau passenger ferry terminal supporting area, and a coastal entertainment and recreation area. . The Taiziwan Project is an important project for the Shekou Industrial Zone to make full use of existing sea areas, shorelines and land resources, strengthen port functions, establish a water passenger transport center, develop an international cruise home port, enhance the image of Shenzhen's modernized international coastal city, and improve the city's functions. .

After the completion of the Prince Edward Bay Cruise Port, it will become the future seaport of Shenzhen and become the largest cruise port in South China. At present, the terminal project of the Shenzhen Taiziwan cruise home port has been completed. With the acceleration of the joint inspection building project, the cruise port mother port is expected to be trial operation in the second half of 2016.

The Shenzhen Municipal Communications Commission also formulated the “Shenzhen Cruise Economy Development Implementation Plan”, which proposed 15 measures from the aspects of improving infrastructure construction and supporting services, promoting the opening of the cruise industry market, optimizing the development environment of the cruise industry, and improving the industrial support policies. .

Sea-rail combined transport to establish a dry port in the inland

The good environment of Shenzhen Port and the advantages of the world's third largest container port have created a strong appeal to the inland provinces. According to reports, Shenzhen Port currently has 47 coastal feeder lines and foreign trade barge routes. The South China Express barge service network covers 49 terminals in the Pan-Pearl River Delta. The number of barge transport containers completed in 2014 was 3.7 million TEUs, a year-on-year increase of 53%. .

The Shenzhen-Tianjin iron-water combined transport route has been extended to Jiangxi, Hubei, Hunan, Yunnan and other places. It has opened 15 sea-rail combined transport lines from Shenzhen to Chongqing, Shaoguan, Changping, Changsha, Chengdu, Kunming, Wuhan and Guiyang. The total volume of containerized iron and water combined transport in Shenzhen port is 172,100 TEU.

In addition, Shenzhen Port has established six inland port cargo terminals and dry ports in Changsha, Zhuzhou, Dongguan Dalang, Kunming, Shaoguan and Hunan Fuling, and plans to set up a dry port in Dongguan Changping Railway Station.
