China's high-speed rail - the world's new calling card

China's high-speed rail proposed construction plan for the first time since 2004, the state council insisted that "the introduction of advanced technology, production design, create Chinese brand" guidelines, to develop the high speed rail business, continuous introduction of foreign advanced high-speed rail technology, strengthen the research and development of independent innovation, improving the core of China's high-speed rail technology, gradually realize the "made in China" to "created in China's development by leaps and bounds.

In the past five years, China's high-speed railway has developed rapidly, and foreign friends who have visited China even praised it as one of the "four great inventions" of China.As a warm and generous country, there are good things to share.Since 2014, China has made remarkable achievements in railway diplomacy.

Chinese railways go global

In 2004, China won the lobito (Angola) -luo (a border town of the democratic republic of Congo) railway, which was completed in August 2014 and covers a total length of 1,344 kilometers.

In January 2011, the abuja-kaduna railway project in Nigeria was launched and completed in November 2014.On November 20, 2014, China railway construction corporation (CRCC) signed a commercial contract for the construction of the Nigerian coastal railway project, with a total length of 186.5 kilometers.

In December 2011, the Ethiopian section was signed, and construction started in April 2012. In January 2012, the djibouti section was signed, and construction started in September 2013. The project is djibouti -- Ethiopia railway, with a total distance of about 770 kilometers.

On November 25, 2013, the total length of the railway from Budapest (capital of Hungary) to Belgrade (capital of Serbia) was 350 kilometers, including 166 kilometers in Hungary and 184 kilometers in Serbia.

On May 11, 2014, the relevant cooperation agreement between Mombasa port and Nairobi railway was signed, with a total length of 480 kilometers.

On August 3, 2014, Thailand's ruling authority approved the railway project connecting China, starting and ending in kunming, China and Singapore, respectively, with 845 kilometers per month.

On November 13, 2015, the cooperation agreement between China and Laos (mainly invested and operated by China) was signed. Kunming, China -- vientiane, Laos, with a total length of 418 kilometers.

China's unique thermal insulation won an order for Indian subways.Across the ocean in Boston, China has received more than 400 subway orders.

China's high-speed rail is changing the world's railway landscape

With the advantages of advanced high-speed railway technology, high safety performance, affordable price and rich operation experience, China's high-speed railway launched an attack on the overseas market, leading China's high-speed railway to the world trend is becoming more and more obvious.China-india cooperation is only the first step for China's high-speed railway to go global. In the future, China's high-speed railway will continue to move forward and cooperate with more countries to improve the overall level of China's high-speed railway and the overall national strength of China.

With the strengthening of the trend of economic globalization, the international cooperation is further strengthened, and the implementation of the "One Belt And One Road" strategy, China's high-speed railway will go overseas with certain foresight.It is expected that in the future, China will pay more and more attention to the opportunities for international cooperation, and enhance the overall level of China's high-speed rail, thereby promoting the improvement of China's overall national strength.It will open up a whole new prospect for China's high-speed railway to the world market.
