Container volume in Jiangyin Port is expected to increase by 10%

(Fuzhou Daily reporter Zhu Yuwen / photo correspondent Chen Ting) Fuzhou News Network March 27th

Fuzhou News Network March 27th (Fuzhou Daily reporter Zhu Yuwen / photo correspondent Chen Ting) On the morning of the 27th, Fuzhou Port Jiangyin Port Area synchronized on-line intelligent tally system, cold chain logistics warehouse, box pipe yard and other three projects, further Optimize the port business environment, promote cost reduction and efficiency, and sprint the first quarter of "opening the door."

The Fuzhou Port Container Intelligent Tally System was officially put into operation, marking the new level of intelligentization of Fuzhou Port. Chen Jing, deputy manager of the Information Department of Fuzhou Port Group, said that tally is to check and hand over the goods when the container arrives at the port. The intelligent tally system automatically detects the high-definition camera on the terminal bridge of the terminal and uses artificial intelligence and other technologies to achieve a correct rate of 97%. Chen Jing said that the system can improve the cargo handling efficiency of imported containers by more than 300%, and the time limit for the delivery of customs tally results messages is shortened from 6 hours to 1 minute after the end of the operation.

The Fujia Cold Chain Warehouse in the core area of Jiangyin Port and the box pipe yard project behind the port area were officially put into operation on the same day.

According to reports, in the first quarter of this year, Fuzhou Port Group is expected to complete cargo throughput of 26.5 million tons, an increase of 11%, and complete container throughput of 735,000 TEUs, an increase of 5.3%. Among them, Jiangyin Port has completed a container volume of 454,000 TEUs. , an increase of 10%.
