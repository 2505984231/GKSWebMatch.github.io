China's first intelligent railway express train independently developed and produced in China

Developed independently by CRRC zhuzhou research institute (hereinafter referred to as "CRRC zhuzhou research institute") and made entirely in China, this is the world's first driverless train.Car in June 2, 2017, zhuzhou, first published by name to "intelligent railway express system (Autonomous rail Rapid Transit - ART)" traffic product, it combines the modern trains and their respective advantages of the new bus transport system belongs to the crossover, it aims to solve urban traffic congestion, make travel difficult people get more choice and experience.

　"Intellectual track" is tailor-made to solve "urban disease".It can supplement a gleam of the universiade city rail transit, also can be used as the passenger's body of lower-tier cities, also can undertake new district to district and center to the tourist routes of transportation, and can be combined with the existing public transportation system fully, build underground, ground and air three-dimensional traffic network, to solve the problem of modern urban transportation problem provides a new solution.
Intelligent rail train, let China's transport leading again

The car can reach speeds of up to 70 kilometers per hour, but because it doesn't rely on steel rails, it can be quickly put into use in as little as a year.The smart rail also has the same advantages of zero emission and no pollution of subway and light rail, and supports a variety of power supply operations.

In the process of operation, the intelligent rail train can also adjust the capacity according to the change of passenger flow. For example, when the standard 3 sections are grouped, the train can carry more than 300 passengers, and when the 5 sections are grouped, it can carry more than 500 passengers, which can effectively solve the problem of small passenger capacity of ordinary buses and greatly improve the capacity.

According to statistics, by 2020, with the acceleration of urbanization, China will add 80 cities with more than one million people every month. However, 80% of small and medium-sized cities cannot afford the expensive construction cost and long construction period of rail transit equipment.By then, in order to solve the problem of urban congestion, nearly 8,000 kilometers of transportation lines with a total investment of more than one trillion yuan should be planned and built, with a huge market potential, among which smart rail is the best choice.
