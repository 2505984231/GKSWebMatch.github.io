Orchard Port Cuntan Port Strategic Integration Adding a “New Engine” to Chongqing Port

The cargo function of Cuntan Port is gradually transferred to Orchard Port

Hualong.com at 6 o'clock on October 13 (trainee Deng Liang) Chongqing, as the only municipality directly under the central government in China, has prominent regional advantages and important strategic position. It is an important strategic fulcrum for the development of the western region. It is located in the “Belt and Road” and the Yangtze River. At the junction of the economic belt. The Orchard Port is a set of sophisticated and important drive wheels at this junction.

Integrating the cargo function of Cuntan Port has become a “new engine” for growth.

In order to make the orchard port of this group turn faster and more powerful, Chongqing Port Logistics Group Co., Ltd. has strategically integrated its Chongqing Orchard Port Container Terminal Co., Ltd. and Chongqing International Container Terminal Co., Ltd.

The freight function of Cuntan Port will be gradually transferred to Orchard Port, and the management mode of “one set of two teams and two brands” will be implemented to help coordinate the development of ports and improve the efficiency of resource utilization.

According to the data of 2017 as of August, the orchard port completed cargo throughput of 7.753 million tons, a year-on-year increase of 10%, and completed container throughput, 228,000 TEU, an increase of 41.9% year-on-year, of which 73,000 boxes of water and water transfer containers were completed. It increased by 125.4% and completed 34,000 containers of hot metal water transport containers, an increase of 118.7%. It has become a "new engine" to promote the growth of container throughput in Chongqing port.

Orchard Port has applied to the country for the establishment of a multimodal transport supervision center

Establishing a multimodal transport standardization system with Singaporean companies

In June of this year, Huilian Group and Figarry Group from Singapore invested 200 million US dollars. The plan is to build a logistics network system that radiates west and east direction with the center of Orchard Port. At the same time, it relies on the distribution of iron, public and water multimodal logistics. The platform and logistics information platform will realize the gathering of freight forwarding companies and special logistics companies with Chongqing as the node.

Not only that, in August this year, Singapore Taiping Shipping, Singapore Port Group and other joint Chongqing Liangjiang Group, Chongqing Port Logistics Group and other establishment of the Sino-Singapore (Chongqing) Interconnection Multimodal Transport Demonstration Project Operation Platform, will build multimodal transport The standardization system, the establishment of intelligent operation system, the use of big data, Internet of Things and other technologies to improve the automation level of multimodal transport and transshipment. The construction of the multimodal transport standardization system will greatly reduce the transshipment cost, so as to give full play to the role of Chongqing Tongjiang Dahai and the connection between Asia and Europe.

According to Xie Jing, director of the Free Trade Office of Chongqing Liangjiang New Area, at present, Orchard Port has applied to the country to establish a multimodal transport supervision center, "to improve the policy function of the orchard port."

Orchard Port in Liangjiang New District is expected to originate in Central Europe (Chongqing) and become the second starting point of Fuxin Europe

It is necessary to have the second originating station of Central Europe (Chongqing), but the lack of function of the foreign trade port of Orchard Port has become a bottleneck restricting its development.

At present, the Liangjiang New Area has applied to the relevant state departments to expand the opening of the Orchard Port, set up designated ports for importing meat, fruits, grain, seedlings, etc., and build a comprehensive open port integrating container, automobile roll-on and bulk cargo. Improve the bonded logistics function of Orchard Port, form a full-scale integrated multimodal transport hub with ports as the node, aviation, railway, highway and waterway seamlessly, and cross-border logistics of China-European class (Chongqing), Yangtze River Golden Waterway and ASEAN land transportation Large channel.

“Application for the Orchard Port is an open port, which is an urgent need for the construction of the Pilot Free Trade Zone and the Sino-Singapore (Chongqing) Strategic Interconnection Demonstration Project.” The relevant person in charge of the Liangjiang New Area said that the Chongqing Free Trade Zone is in urgent need of the scale of the water transport port. Advantages, development of an open economy. At the same time, the inland provinces such as Sichuan, Shaanxi and Hubei around Chongqing also carry the heavy responsibility of the construction of the third batch of free trade pilot zones. It is also urgent to require high-throughput water transport ports to realize the entry and exit of free trade goods.

This year, Liangjiang New District and Fuxinou (Chongqing) Logistics Co., Ltd. have signed a strategic framework agreement, the two sides will jointly build a China-European (Chongqing) multimodal transport project, and plan to build a Sino-European (Chongqing) international cargo assembly in Orchard Port. The center will build a Chongqing transit platform for international trade cooperation between China and Europe (Chongqing). By then, the Orchard Port in the Liangjiang New District is expected to become the second starting point for the Central European train (Chongqing).

Orchard Port has more than doubled in 3 years

On February 5 this year, the Chongqing Municipal People's Government issued the "13th Five-Year Plan" for the development of Chongqing's modern logistics industry. It is clear in the plan that it will rely on the largest orchard port intermodal hub port, the Yangtze River Golden Waterway and the Sino-Singapore (Chongqing) Intermodal Multimodal Transport Demonstration Project and water transport port in the inland river to exert the advantages of iron, public and water transport and free trade test zones. It integrates functions such as multimodal transport, warehousing and distribution, bonded trade and logistics to build a shipping logistics hub upstream of the Yangtze River.

According to the rules, three years later, in 2020, the orchard port will reach 3.8 square kilometers (and the initial planning area of Orchard Port will be 4 square kilometers), of which the new material storage land area is 3.74 square kilometers.

In the future, Orchard Port will expand the economic hinterland through port city linkage and port city interaction, promote the coordinated development of ports, industries and cities, and create a new bright spot for the construction of the “Belt and Road” meeting point, and comprehensively enhance its core competitiveness.
