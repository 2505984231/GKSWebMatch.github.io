Detailed introduction

The Hangzhou Bay Bridge is a cross-sea bridge that spans the Hangzhou Bay in China.The bridge starts from Zhengjiatun, Haiyan, Jiaxing City, Zhejiang Province, and Cixi Waterway Bay, Ningbo, south, with a total length of 36 kilometers.


The Hangzhou Bay Bridge was opened to traffic on May 1, 2008.The bridge is designed according to the standard of two-way six-lane expressway and is an important part of the G15 Shenhai Expressway.


The Hangzhou Bay Bridge has maintained the world record for the World Record Association's longest sea-crossing bridge in China.The Hangzhou Bay Bridge is the fourth longest sea-crossing bridge in the world after the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge, the Pontchartrain Lake Bridge in the United States and the Jiaozhou Bay Bridge in Qingdao, China.


The construction of the bridge is conducive to actively connecting to Shanghai, expanding opening up, promoting cooperation and exchanges in the Yangtze River Delta region, improving the level of opening up to the outside world in Zhejiang, especially Ningbo and Jiaxing, enhancing comprehensive strength and international competitiveness; and conducive to improving the Yangtze River Delta region.The layout of the road network and the main road of the national highway will alleviate the pressure of the traffic flow of the Shanghai, Hangzhou and Ningbo expressways; it will help to change the situation of the traffic end of Ningbo, and thus become a transportation hub, implement the development strategy of the Hangzhou Bay area; help promote the river and ZhejiangThe needs of Shanghai tourism development.


overall arrangement

The Hangzhou Bay Sea-crossing Bridge consists of the Haizhong Platform, the North-South Channel Kong Bridge, the Water District Approach Bridge, the Tidal Area Approach Bridge, the Land Area Approach Bridge, each bridge tower and each interchange crossing. The whole bridge section is arranged from northwest to southeast.


Construction features

The Hangzhou Bay Bridge uses the Wuyue culture concept of Zhejiang, Shanghai and Jiangsu.In the bridge type, the designer adopts the form of the West Lake Sudi, integrating transportation and sightseeing.To balance the water in Hangzhou BayThe characteristics of the text environment, "long bridge lying wave" design will outline the bridge plane into a S-shaped curve, beautiful, lively bridge type allows drivers and passengers to enjoy the pleasure of driving and riding.And if you build a straight road, it is easy to cause visual fatigue.


Hangzhou Bay is one of the three strong tide bays in the world. There are tornadoes formed by typhoons and microclimates, with chaotic flow rates and flow directions.The design of "Long Bridge" is also due to the safety of the bridge, and it has reserved channels for the Qiantang River and the passing ships.


research innovation

The high technological content of the Hangzhou Bay Bridge is first reflected in the construction process. The bridge design adopts the prefabrication, factory, large-scale, and offshore construction as the construction plan for onshore construction, breaking through the long-term design decision-making concept. The largest component of prefabricated hoisting is prestressed concrete box girder with a length of 70 meters, a width of 16 meters, a height of 4.0 meters and a weight of 2180 tons. The longest member is an ultra-long steel pipe pile with a length of 84 meters and a diameter of 1.6 meters. Can be said to be unparalleled in the world. In order to reduce the corrosion of chloride ions in seawater to the steel and concrete of the bridge and ensure the life of the bridge for 100 years, the designer has specially developed a set of effective schemes to prevent seawater corrosion. These visible bridge projects have high technological content. The Hangzhou Bay Bridge will be a "digital bridge." The scientific research unit will use a hardware and interface technology, network and database technology, image graphics technology, artificial intelligence technology, computational mathematics, finite element technology, mechanics and other multidisciplinary to establish a scientific evaluation system for bridge design, construction and maintenance. The bridge will be equipped with a central surveillance system with an average of 1 pair of monitors per 1 km. In this way, not only can the bridge be scientifically and reasonably maintained, but the operation status of the bridge is also in real time. In 2012, the project has reported 17 key research projects for the bridge project to the Ministry of Communications, which is rare in the domestic bridge industry.。


The construction conditions of the Hangzhou Bay Bridge are very harsh. To ensure the safety and quality of offshore construction, design and construction must be considered comprehensively. After many domestic and foreign research and expert consultation, the overall principle of construction decision design was formulated, the time of offshore operation was minimized, and the offshore construction was changed to onshore construction. The design and construction principles of factoryization, large-scale and mechanization were adopted.


The Hangzhou Bay Bridge uses the design, manufacture, anti-corrosion and construction technology of large-diameter super-long steel pipe piles. The bridge steel pipe pile foundation has the characteristics of pile length, large diameter and huge quantity. The pile is 89 meters long and the pile diameter is 1.5 meters and 1.6 meters, for a total of 5474.


Meaning impact

The completion of the Hangzhou Bay Bridge has promoted the economy of Hangzhou and accelerated the integration of Ningbo and Taizhou into the Yangtze River Delta. It has played a very good role in promoting the development of Zhejiang. The development of Zhejiang has also played a certain role in the provincial capital of Hangzhou. enhancement. Hangzhou Bay is located in the Yangtze River Delta, where China's reform and opening up is the most dynamic and economically developed. The construction of the Hangzhou Bay Bridge has far-reaching and significant strategic significance for the economic and social development of the entire region.
