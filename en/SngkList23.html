Zhanjiang Port exceeded 20 million in 2018

(Event site)

With the official deputy secretary of the municipal government Liu Bing officially announced: Zhanjiang Port exceeded 1 million TEU in 2018! The “New Zhanjiang” round of COSCO Shipping and the “Renjian 5” round of Antong Holdings sing in unison, the 1 millionth standard box was slowly lifted to the “New Zhanjiang” wheel, and the container throughput of Zhanjiang Port exceeded 100 for the first time in 2018. Wan box. This marks that Zhanjiang Port has entered the ranks of the million-standard container port and opened a new chapter in the development of container business in Zhanjiang Port.

(Gift)

(lifting the 1 millionth standard)

At the follow-up meeting of the 2017 Annual Container Support Awards, Comrade Wang Aidian introduced the development of port production and construction. It is also the key to encourage the container to break through 1 million.

(Container support rewards will be cashed on site)

Then, the head of the city's transportation bureau, Mr. Luo Lie, announced the list of winners, and the representatives of the company gave their acceptance speeches.

(Speaking by representatives of the winning companies)

(Award ceremony)

In this meeting, Liu Bing, deputy secretary-general of Zhanjiang Municipal Government, pointed out that Zhanjiang Port has thoroughly implemented Zhanjiang City's “Port Revitalization” strategy. With its unique geographical advantages, the container business has made breakthroughs in recent years. Since the operation of Baoman Container Terminal in 2013, the route network has been continuously improved, basically forming a direct liner network system covering the domestic coastal areas and Southeast Asia and Northeast Asia. In 2016, the municipal government issued the document “Zhanjiang Municipal People's Government Office to further increase incentives to promote Zhanjiang Port. The Notice of the Development of the Container Transportation Industry Boosts the Port. At the end of the speech, Liu Bing enthusiastically encouraged entrepreneurs to seize the opportunity and go hand in hand to create a goal for Zhanjiang Port to become a container hub port in the Beibu Gulf.

(Comrade Liu Bing speaks)

At present, Zhanjiang Port has opened 40 container routes, including 25 domestic trade routes, which basically cover the main coastal ports of the country; 15 foreign trade routes, 13 ports of 7 countries (regions) have been linked to the port; the container sea-rail combined transport train 17 has been opened. Articles, outlets cover the Yunnan, Guizhou and other regions, forming a smooth channel of international land and sea logistics. The container business of Zhanjiang Port has a good momentum of development. The annual throughput has maintained double-digit growth. In just 4 years, the historical span of throughput from 500,000 TEUs to 1 million TEUs has been achieved.

Since 2014, the municipal government has introduced a special support and reward policy for supporting container development, and its support has been continuously increased. In 2016, the municipal government raised the award standard to 30 million yuan. In 2017, the incentive fund allocation plan was optimized. The government's support has greatly boosted the confidence of customers such as port and cargo, and has driven the rapid growth of the hinterland supply. The container business has maintained a steady and rapid development momentum.

Wang Aimian, assistant to the president of Zhanjiang Port Group and general manager of the container company, said that Zhanjiang Port will take the realization of one million standard containers as a new starting point. With the strong support of the municipal party committee and municipal government, it will fully seize the Zhanjiang City docking Hainan Free Trade Zone and China-ASEAN. The trade zone and the construction of the port-type national logistics hub carry the city. China Merchants and Zhanjiang City have comprehensively deepened the "1+N" strategic cooperation and the Zhanjiang Port Group's implementation of the five major development strategies and other major opportunities, actively integrated into the international land and sea trade new channel construction, and accelerated the promotion of Bao The first phase of the expansion of the container terminal project, the integration of industry resources to create a modern logistics supply chain platform, etc., continuously enhance the external influence and comprehensive competitiveness, consolidate the status of the transit port of Zhanjiang Port, and strive to create a new era of high-quality development in the era of innovation.

It is understood that the first phase expansion project of Zhanjiang Port Baoman Container Terminal, which is currently being promoted, is a provincial and municipal key project, and two 50,000-ton container berths will be constructed (water structure is designed and constructed according to 150,000 tons). After the project is completed, it will form an overall scale effect with the existing terminal. By then, the total length of the dock shoreline will reach 1,324 meters, and many 12,500 TEU large container ships can be berthed at the same time. The annual throughput capacity of the terminal reaches 2 million TEUs, and the development potential and prospects have attracted much attention.
