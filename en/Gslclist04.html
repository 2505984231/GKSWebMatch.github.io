  • Suspension monorail

China's fastest suspended monorail train has rolled off the line, with a top speed of 70 kilometers per hour.

Suspension monorail, commonly known as "empty rail", is a monorail system.Unlike normal rail transit, the track is above the train, supported in the air by steel or cement columns, and the train is suspended below the rail beam.As a light, medium-speed and medium-volume public transportation system, it has the advantages of green environmental protection, low cost, less land use, low operating noise, strong adaptability, all-weather operation and so on. It also has the function of sightseeing and is a unique mode of transportation in urban public transportation and scenic area transportation.

The suspension monorail train developed by CRRC Qingdao sifang ltd. has completely independent intellectual property rights.

Liu yuwen, deputy director of the technology center of China railway rolling stock co., LTD., said that the suspended monorail trains designed and manufactured by China railway rolling stock corp.The train is designed to run at 80 kilometers per hour and has a top speed of 70 kilometers per hour, which is comparable to the subway and is the highest empty rail train in China.

"The monorail USES a new permanent magnet motor, which features high power, small size, low noise and light weight to achieve better operating efficiency."Liu yuwen said that because of its high power and light weight, the suspension monorail has a good climbing ability, with a maximum climbing capacity of 100%.(the height of the train rises 100 meters after 1000 meters forward), which is more than three times of the climbing ability of ordinary subway vehicles. It can operate flexibly in rugged and steep mountainous areas, and guarantee a high safety and comfort level.

The track of the suspended monorail train is directly above the vehicle, and it is not affected by rain or snow, nor does it have derailment risk. The train has a variety of active and passive safety devices, such as whole-vehicle monitoring system, battery rescue and combined rescue.In addition, the track curve radius of the suspended monorail is small, which takes up less ground space, and the demolition works can be significantly reduced, saving the cost investment, which is only one third of the cost of subway.Compared with ground trams, suspension cars avoid the problem of mixing with ground vehicles, and have the advantages of safety, punctuality and strong capacity to carry passengers.
