China's high-speed maglev prototype rolled off the production line in Qingdao

On May 23, 2019, CRRC rolled off the production line in Qingdao, China. Ding sansan, deputy chief engineer of CRRC sifang, said the market for high-speed maglev trains running at 600 kilometers per hour is aimed at filling the speed gap between air and rail traffic. "Between cities traveling 1,000 and 1,500 kilometers, high speed maglev will be Best transportation option. In addition, ding said maglev trains have a speed advantage over high-speed trains, which can cover nearly 20 kilometers in three minutes. For example, going from 0 to 600 kilometers per hour, Maglev trains have an acceleration time of three minutes and 20 kilometers, while high-speed trains speed up to 350 kilometers and take six minutes and 25 kilometers. But according to CRRC's plan, it won't run the test line until around 2022.

　Maglev train is to use electromagnetic force to offset the gravity of the earth, through automatic control means to maintain a certain gap between the train body and the track (suspension gap is about 1 cm), so that the train suspended on the track. Compared with ordinary wheel-rail train, it has the characteristics of low noise, small vibration, no pollution, small turning radius, strong climbing ability and easy implementation, and has the reputation of "zero altitude aircraft". Its research and manufacture involve many subjects such as automatic control theory, sensor technology, power electronics technology, linear propulsion technology, mechanism dynamics, network communication, fault monitoring and diagnosis, etc. The technology is very complex, which is an important symbol of national scientific and technological strength and industrial level.
China's first medium-low speed maglev line has become the fourth country to master medium-low speed maglev technology

In March 2010, member of the project by five and seven authoritative expert review acceptance, experts believe that China has mastered the low-speed maglev transportation system in lightweight suspension control, traction control, train, rail beam optimization, F type of rolling guide rail, rail system, operation control, system design and integration of core and key technologies, and mastered in the low speed maglev traffic system technology,It has reached the world's advanced level.

　The medium-low speed maglev traffic test and demonstration line at the north-controlled tangshan test base is 1.5 kilometers long, 2 meters wide, designed with a slope of 70‰ and a radius of 50 meters. The maximum speed of two maglev trains can reach 105 kilometers.The maglev train is almost noiseless and runs smoothly.Professor li jie, director of the maglev engineering research center at the national university of defense science and technology, told reporters that the maglev train is 3 meters wide and 14 meters long, with a maximum effective carrying capacity of 15 tons.
