China's first permanent magnet straddle monorail train rolled off the line, reaching an international leading level.

The first permanent magnet straddle-type monorail train developed by CRRC sixi co., ltd. is an internationally leading dual-axle bogies based on permanent magnet traction with large traffic volume and straddle-type monorail, with prominent advantages such as "strong climbing ability, small turning radius, large passenger capacity, safety and reliability, low noise, energy conservation and environmental protection".

According to operational requirements, the car can be flexibly grouped into four sections, six sections and eight sections. The car body width is 2.9 meters, the total length of six sections is 90.8 meters, and it can carry 1,448 passengers. It can be called the "hercules" of straddle monorail trains.The maximum running speed of the vehicle is 80 km/h and the service life of the vehicle is as long as 30 years.

In addition to leading technology, the car also has a beautiful appearance.Low resistance streamlined head, with purple ribbons running through the body, brings a strong sense of speed and visual impact.Each car has 3 pairs of doors, which shortens the loading and unloading time.The connecting part of the carriage is designed as barrier-free through passage, which makes the passage more convenient.In addition, the car is also the first to use a new nano ceramic film Windows, can isolate ultraviolet rays, partition 97% of the infrared, ride more comfortable.Air conditioning air ducts are sprayed with antibacterial and antiviral nanomaterials to provide passengers with a healthier air environment.The new TPU floor cloth is used in the guest room, which has the advantages of wear-resisting, self-cleaning and environmental protection.

The biggest highlight of the straddle monorail is the use of permanent magnet synchronous motor drive for the first time, energy saving advantages.It is estimated that compared with monorail vehicles with asynchronous motors, the vehicle can save more than 10% energy.At the same time, the vehicle has a strong climbing ability and a small turning radius. It can pass 65‰ of ramps and lines with a curve radius of 50 meters. It is highly adaptable to complex topography and geology.Low operating noise. When the train travels at 70km/h, the noise at the distance of 5 meters from the center line of the track is only 70dB, "lower than the noise generated by cars on the road".

Straddle type monorail commonly known as "skytrain", is a very distinctive rail transit mode, characterized by its unconventional track of two balanced tracks, but a single belt of reinforced concrete beam body, vehicles with rubber tires, riding straddle on the rail beam.

The straddle monorail train has been independently developed by CRRC sixi co., ltd. for three years, and has mastered key technologies independently. The domestic production rate of the whole vehicle has reached over 95%, and the track beam and switch system supporting the vehicle have also been domestic.Therefore, CRRC sifang co., ltd. can cooperate with relevant domestic enterprises to provide users with a complete set of straddle-type monorail system solutions.

With the continuous maturity and improvement of technology, straddle-type monorail is popular in various countries in the world, which is widely used in urban backbone, auxiliary line, airport connecting line, sightseeing line and so on.According to incomplete statistics, more than 50 straddle monorail lines have been opened in the world.In China, chongqing was the first city to introduce the straddle monorail. Its 55 km chongqing rail transit line 3 is the longest monorail line in the world.Many cities in China have also been planned or have planning intention.Therefore, the application prospect of straddle monorail is broad.
