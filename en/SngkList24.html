Successfully completed the comprehensive emergency drill of Nanjing Port Terminal in 2019

On the morning of June 26, hosted by Jiangsu Provincial Department of Transportation and Jiangsu Port Group Co., Ltd., Jiangsu Provincial Transportation Comprehensive Administrative Law Enforcement Supervision Bureau, Nanjing Transportation Bureau, Nanjing Port (Group) Co., Ltd. The comprehensive emergency drill of the dangerous goods terminal was successfully held in Nanjing Port Co., Ltd. Di Feng, deputy general manager of Nanjing Port Group, was the chief commander, deputy general manager Gu Guohua, safety director Xu Yuezong as deputy commander, and general manager of Nanjing Port Co., Ltd. served as the site commander.

Zhou Zhimu, director of the Water Law Enforcement Supervision Bureau of Jiangsu Provincial Transportation Comprehensive Administrative Law Enforcement Supervision Bureau, Shen Weixin, member of the Party Committee and Deputy General Manager of Jiangsu Port Group, Yan Hui, deputy director of Nanjing Transportation Bureau, Nanjing Maritime Safety Administration, Nanjing Branch of Changhang Public Security Bureau, Leaders of the Nanjing Municipal Administration Office, Yangzhou Yizheng Ecological Environment Bureau, Yangzhou Chemical Industry Park Environmental Protection Bureau and other units visited the on-site command. The leaders of the Jiangsu Port Group Safety and Environmental Protection Department and the subordinate units of the Jiangsu Port Group were in charge of safety leadership and safety department heads to observe and practice on the spot.

The entire training organization is rigorously arranged, coordinated and smooth, targeted and practical, fully demonstrating the ability and level of local and enterprises to cope with major accidents, and further improving the emergency linkage mechanism of enterprises and relevant government departments, strengthening coordination and cooperation, and improving The level of emergency management has been usefully explored and provided valuable experience.
