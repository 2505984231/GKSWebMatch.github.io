Xu Peixing

Xu Peixing is an outstanding leader in Shanghai. The Huangpu River, which was originally developed in Shanghai, has become a bottleneck restricting the great rejuvenation of China's international shipping center. In order to change the situation that a large number of goods need to be transferred from Busan and Tokyo, the state will take advantage of the opportunity of the Shanghai Port Policy to support Yangshan Port and end the history of Shanghai without deep water. In view of its idea of “taking Hong Kong to develop the city, lending the city to Hong Kong, connecting with the United States, becoming stronger and better” and actively promoting the revitalization of China’s international shipping center, it is regarded as the “designer” of China’s port management industry in 2006.

Related port

Personal experience

In the second half of 2008, Xu Peixing just dismissed the Shanghai Port Authority. The financial crisis was transmitted to Shanghai Port. Foreign trade orders fell sharply, and port throughput immediately bottomed out. Shanghai Port also stood at the turning point of history. He feels that the model of Shanghai International Shipping Center is not a replica of any existing and relatively fast-developing shipping center. It has its own characteristics. He rewrote the pattern of the world's shipping center, a designer with a global vision to develop the great renaissance of China's international shipping center.

Honors

He successively served as the captain of the Traffic Police Corps of the Shanghai Public Security Bureau, the Deputy Director of the Shanghai Public Security Bureau (1996.11-1999.8), the Deputy Director of the Shanghai Public Utilities Administration (1999.8-2000.4), and the Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Transportation Administration (2000.4-2001.4). Deputy Director of the Shanghai Construction and Management Committee (2001.4-2003.1). He has been a visiting scholar at American universities and graduated from East China Normal University with a major in world economics.

Contribution

Xu Peixing was appointed Director of the Shanghai Port Authority in January 2003. Mr. Xu Peixing, the head of Shanghai Water Conservancy Promotion Department, took the opportunity of CEPA implementation to promote cooperation between Shanghai and Hong Kong and visited Zhaoxiang Company. Ask questions and comments about the harbour, solve problems and make useful suggestions. The fifth and sixth generation container ships have become mainstream. The Huangpu River, originally developed for the development of Shanghai Port, has become a bottleneck restricting the great rejuvenation of China's shipping center.
