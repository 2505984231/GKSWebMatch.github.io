Guangzhou Port and Yunfu jointly promote logistics network

The reporter learned from the Guangzhou Port Group that on July 4, the group signed a framework agreement on port cooperation with the Yunfu Municipal Government. The two sides will conduct in-depth cooperation in the areas of port shoreline resource development, infrastructure construction, logistics network and industrial development. Promote industrial integration, complementary advantages and cooperative development of Guangzhou City and Yunfu City.

According to the agreement, Yunfu Municipal Government will cooperate with the port enterprises supporting Guangzhou Port Group and the port enterprises of Yunfu City to jointly promote the development and utilization of the planned coastline resources of Yunfu Port and further enhance the port infrastructure service capacity of Yunfu Port. Yunfu Port has become an inland river hub for the Xijiang Golden Waterway and has become a new engine for Yunfu City to develop the economic belt around the Pearl River Delta.

Guangzhou Port Group will play its role as a core hub port and a perfect logistics network resource, accelerate the expansion and upgrading of Yunfu Port, improve quality and efficiency, reduce logistics costs, become the core support for accelerating industrial development, and promote the integration of Yunfu City into Guangdong.
