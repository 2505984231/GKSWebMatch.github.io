approached “Fuxing bullet train”

"Fuhsing" From 300km/h to 350km/h to witness "China speed"


Named "fuxing," the reason for the decisive victory in the building of a moderately prosperous society in all respects, the realization of the Chinese peopleAs an important historical node of the Chinese dream of great rejuvenation, the standard bullet train group of China is named "fuxing", which can truly record the technical equipment of China's high-speed railwayThe development to the advanced ranks of the world is deeply embodied in the pursuit and expectation of Chinese railway people for the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation."The fuxing is hereThe beijing-shanghai high-speed railway is ripe and ready to operate at 350 kilometers per hour. This is a decision made by the railway authorities to implement the central government's supply-side structural reformThis is an important symbol of China's high-speed railway showing the world's leading standards.


Since the 18th national congress of the communist party of China (CPC), great achievements have been made in the development of China's railway, especially high-speed railway, which has become the world's largest railwayThe country with the largest scale of high-speed railway construction and operation, the most comprehensive technology and rich management experience.By the end of 2016, China had built a high speed of world modernizationThe railway network, the operating mileage of high-speed railway has exceeded 22,000 kilometers, ranking first in the world.At present, the number of high-speed bullet trains in China has reached more than 2,700, running every dayMore than 4,500 bullet trains have carried more than 5 million passengers, and the number of high-speed trains has exceeded 6 billion.More importantly, China has it all figured outThe company has developed the key and core technologies of high-speed railway, and achieved a series of independent innovation achievements in the fields of high-speed railway engineering construction, high-speed bullet train set, train control, traction power supply, operation management and safety assurance.


Fuxing's growth history

2017

June 26, 2017

The fuxing, China's standard bullet train, will start in both directions at Beijing south railway station and Shanghai hongqiao railway station."Fuxing" is a bullet train set of Chinese standards, which is led and developed by China railway corporation and has completely independent intellectual property rights and reached the world's advanced level.

August 21, 2017

"Fuxing" China standard bullet trains are running on the beijing-tianjin inter-city line, reaching 10 stations in south Beijing, wuqing, tianjin and west Beijing.

September 21, 2017

The fuxing bullet trains began their first day of operation on the beijing-shanghai high-speed rail line, which is speeding up to 350 kilometers per hour.From that day on, seven pairs of fuxing China standard bullet trains will travel every day from Beijing south to Shanghai hongqiao, a journey that can take as little as four hours and 24 minutes.

December 28, 2017

The "first show" of "fuxing" in southwest China.On the same day, the national railway system began to implement the new train operation map.The G1372 train from kunming south to Shanghai hongqiao has become the first bullet train to carry passengers in southwest China.

2018

July 1, 2018

Sixteen "fuxing" bullet trains were unveiled on the beijing-shanghai high-speed railway.Compared with the previous 8 "fuxing" trains, the new trains have the same top speed, added functions such as adjustable lighting and USB charging port, and also increased the number of business seats and the capacity of the whole train.

August 1, 2018

The beijing-tianjin inter-city railway has been replaced with "fuxing" bullet trains, allowing passengers to enjoy wi-fi services throughout the journey.

December 24, 2018

"Blue sunshine boy" fuxing, a 250km/h CR300BF bullet train, can be applied to different routes with different infrastructures and different passenger flows. With the advantages of low energy consumption and high cost performance, it can meet various transport service needs.

2019

January 5, 2019

The hulk fuxing is operating at 160 kilometers per hour.Some high-speed lines will enter the era of bullet trains.The cabin has full wi-fi coverage, the sleeping berth has a USB power port, and there are seats and toilets for disabled people.

At 12:00 on January 5, 2019

The G9 train from Beijing south to Shanghai hongqiao station left on time, which is also the 17-section ultra-long version of "fuxing" that will be launched nationwide after the map is adjusted.
