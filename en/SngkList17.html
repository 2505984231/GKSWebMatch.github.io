Xiamen multimodal port station officially opened to effectively link sea and land logistics

Xiamen multimodal port station officially launched the launch ceremony.


The launching ceremony of the official operation of the Xiamen multimodal port station.


From the Xiamen Port Group multimodal transport port station, the first sea-rail combined transport class was 76,636 trains.


According to reports, this class is responsible for the purchase of clothes, shoes, hats, timber and other goods imported from abroad through the "Haisi" route. After the goods are docked from the port of Xiamen, the sea line is passed by the Haishu Station railway line. The mode of intermodal transportation was shipped to Ganzhou, Jiangxi.

The opening of the multi-modal port of Xiamen Port Group is the joint operation of the China Railway Nanchang Bureau Group Co., Ltd. Zhangzhou Vehicle Section and Xiamen Port Group, which will continue to promote the construction of the “Belt and Road” brand “Silk Road Shipping” and promote the development of Xiamen Port multimodal transport business. It is an important measure to promote the seamless connection between “Haisi” and “Lusi” transportation. The port station is located behind the berth at No. 7 in Haishu Port Area, adjacent to the Haishu Railway Freight Yard. It has a unique geographical advantage and covers an area of ??about 15,000 square meters. It can accommodate 17 railway container wagons at one time, and its annual processing capacity exceeds 50,000 TEU. As a public service platform, the multimodal transport port station project mainly undertakes the entry and exit operations of the sea-rail intermodal container of Haigang Port Area of ??Xiamen Port. Its operation will further expand the capacity of Xiamen Port to collect and transport, and open up the port of sea-rail intermodal container. "The last mile." As a result, the “Xiamen-Jiangxi” sea-rail combined transport corridor will be more smooth, and it will play a positive role in optimizing the allocation of railway transportation resources, improving the efficiency of port collection and distribution, reducing port logistics costs, and guiding the flow of goods in the hinterland.

According to statistics, the CEIBS (Xiamen) class has a total of 348 lines, which are sent to more than 10 countries in Central Europe and Central Asia through the port stations of Alashankou, Manzhouli and Erlianhot. There are 147 foreign trade routes in Xiamen Port, reaching 44 countries and regions around the world. 138 ports. Among them, Xiamen has opened 57 “One Belt, One Road” sea routes and 20 air routes, forming a seamless connection “One Belt and One Road” logistics channel.

As a kind of transportation mode with high capacity, low cost, high efficiency and safety, railway transportation has been well received by customers. In recent years, China Railway Nanchang Bureau Group Co., Ltd. Zhangzhou Vehicle Section and Xiamen Port Group Corporation have joined forces. Xiamen Port's multimodal transport business has grown from scratch to small and large. Xiamen to Jiangxi inland and other places have been running a number of sea and rail transport trains. The development of Xiamen Port multimodal transport business has effectively reduced the logistics costs of production enterprises and effectively reduced the pollution of the port cargo transportation to the environment, and strongly supported The development of Xiamen's port economy will accelerate the construction of sea-rail combined transport channels and gradually form a logistics network system based on seaports and railways, which will greatly promote the economic cooperation between the inland provinces and cities and Xiamen. .

At present, raw materials such as minerals and wood plates from Southeast Asia, Africa and Europe have been transported to the inland provinces such as Jiangxi by sea-rail combined transport in Xiamen Port; and the inland areas have frequently produced goods such as bags and furniture. Exported from Xiamen Port to the rest of the world by means of sea-rail combined transport.

It is also known that since Xiamen Port took the lead in the "Silk Road Shipping" in December last year, the various logistics methods at sea and on the land have been organically connected, gradually forming an open pattern of linkage between the land and the sea and between the east and the west. As of May 2019, Xiamen Central Europe trains sent more than 200,000 tons, achieving transportation income of more than 200 million yuan; Xiamen Port and sea rail transport completed 15,000 TEUs, an increase of 101.18%.

More than 50 shipping, port, land port, freight and supply chain management companies such as China Railway Nanchang Bureau Group Co., Ltd., Xiamen Port Group Corporation and Russia Far East actively responded to the construction of “Silk Road Shipping” and made efforts to build Fujian Province to take the lead in proposing and practicing. The sea-rail intermodal service brand with specific service standards is committed to building a “One Belt, One Road” international trade shipping service platform with standardized identification, convenient operation and intelligent management. This will serve the overall development of the “Belt and Road” initiative. Further exert the advantages and functions of the core area of “Haisi” in Fujian Province, and promote the “One Belt and One Road” to build a deeper and deeper reality.
