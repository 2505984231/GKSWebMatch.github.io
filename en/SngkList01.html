Chang Dechuan

Chairman of Qingdao Port (Group) Co., Ltd. An innovative entrepreneur is deeply loved by the staff of Qingdao Port. Chang Dechuan is a world-class port manager and a banner for the development of Chinese ports. Being responsible, being strict with self-discipline, being highly respected, being amiable, being approachable, and caring for the hardships of workers is a true portrayal of their personal influence and personal charisma. The affinity and cohesiveness he released was reflected in the professionalism of the staff of Qingdao Port. In view of its keen sense of port management, the management system and method with Chinese characteristics suitable for the development of Qingdao Port is known as the “godfather” of China's port management industry in 2006.

Personal experience

Chang Dechuan insisted that "the revolutionary ideal is higher than the heavens, and that the morality is strong and dedication", insisting on "always listening to the party, always going with the party, always reporting the party's grace", loyally practicing the scientific development concept, and building a century-old port into three modernizations. Xingang has made Qingdao Port advance to the top seven in the world big port from a small port with a throughput of only 20 million tons. His practice of reforming and developing state-owned enterprises for 40 years has created "Changde's Opinions on State-owned Enterprises", which has been praised by experts as: Leading Qingdao Port with practice, using facts, using data, and even using international standards for judging whether state-owned enterprises can do well, The entrepreneurs made the most affirmative answer and stood up to the backbone of the Chinese nation. Won the national model worker, the national outstanding entrepreneurs and other titles.

Honors

Chang Dechuan is a professor at Shanghai Maritime University and an expert in the “Special Government Allowance” of the State Council. With excellent management performance, it has won the national model worker, the national outstanding entrepreneur, the first "Yuan Baohua Enterprise Management Gold Award", the first "entrepreneurial entrepreneur" in the country, the first top ten "China outstanding quality people", and the national quality management outstanding contributors The most creative Chinese business leaders in the Asia-Pacific region, the top ten news figures in China's reform in the new century, the top ten basic figures in China's 30 years of reform and opening up, the 30-year meritorious deeds of China's Lushang, and the Outstanding Contribution Award of China's energy-saving and emission reduction meritorious deeds, Honorary title of “Outstanding Managers for Customer Satisfaction in China” and “Outstanding Managers for the 30th Anniversary of Total Quality Management in China”.

Contribution

Chang Dechuan led the Qingdao people to "respect their loyalty to the country, serve the society, and benefit the workers". During the 30 years of reform and opening up, they created more than 20 billion yuan of excellent assets for the country, and the total amount of taxes paid by the state was more than 15 billion yuan, which is equivalent to contributing to the country. More than 20 Qingdao Ports in the late 1980s. Since the "10th Five-Year Plan", it has created a source of revenue of 196.1 billion yuan for the country. Various taxes and fees were paid 11.47 billion yuan, and the city tax was paid first in five years. Leading a group of people in Qingdao Port to complete the large-scale construction of container terminals, 200,000-ton ore terminals, oil terminals and coal terminals during the “10th Five-Year Plan” period, and to undertake the explosive growth of cargo throughput of the Yellow River and Bohai Bay. Chang Dechuan has carried out scientific and technological innovations for national effectiveness, such as EDI and other projects.
